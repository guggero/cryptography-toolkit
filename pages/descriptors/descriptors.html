Output Descriptors

Explanation

Paste a BIP-380 output descriptor to inspect it and derive addresses. The descriptor is parsed on every keystroke through btcutil-js's WASM descriptors module, so the checksum, stats and derived addresses always reflect what you typed.

Any existing #checksum suffix is stripped and recomputed, so you can paste a descriptor with a missing or outdated checksum and read the correct canonical form back out.

Sources:

  • BIP-380 specification
  • btcutil-js library
